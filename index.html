3D Space Pathfinding
Perfect for AI in space or plane simulator.

Since classic NavMesh works only with plain surfaces, it's not suitable for open 3D space. You can solve this problem using 3D Space Pathfinding, which provides you with a drop-in solution for pathfinding in 3D space.
